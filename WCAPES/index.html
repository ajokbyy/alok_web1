HOW IT WORK

The Company will be responsible for buying the rights of emerging players in the youth categories. It will be funded initially by the private and public sale of the WCA token.


These acquired rights will make it possible to stipulate sponsorship contracts for players for a period previously defined by the contract referred to above.


The company will have 31% of management represented by people from the world of football who already carry out similar activities. The relevant decisions will be taken by a board of directors of which 20% will be part of the company owned by the founders of the project and the remaining 49% by a tokenized company where the decision-making power will be up to the will of the voting rights on a platform.

DAO created ad hoc, which will allow holders of tokens per share to vote.
